Keyboard instruction manual

1:-> To exit the control page and release all resources (2 times quickly).
2:-> To start or stop the face recognition mode.
3:-> To attach or release the carry tray.
4:-> To move the device forward and to the left.
5:-> To move the device forward.
6:-> To move the device forward and to the right.
7:-> To move the device to the left.
8:-> To move the device to the right.
9:-> To move the device back and to the left.
10:-> To move the device backward.
11:-> To move the device back and to the right.
12 (5, 14):-> To activate the normal mode of movement and use the mouse to turn left or right.
13:-> To pause the alert sound.
14:-> To control the view.
15:-> To refresh the control page for reconnecting to the live streaming.

Joystick instruction manual

1:-> To move all direction control.
2:-> To control the view.
3:-> To exit the control page and release all resources (2 times quickly).
4:-> To start or stop the face recognition mode.
5:-> To pause the alert sound.
6:-> To refresh the control page for reconnecting to the live streaming.
7:-> To attach or release the carry tray.
8 (1, 2):-> To activate the normal mode of movement and use the view stick to turn left or right.
